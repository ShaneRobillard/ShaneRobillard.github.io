About Me

My Library

I have a enormous library of at least 700+ books between my wife and I. We have 10 and soon to be 11 bookcases that are full of books.

Best Times

My favorite time to read is at night right before going to sleep. It helps me calm down and relax. This helps me fall asleep. My dreams are usually calmer and more vivid after a good hour of reading.

There are plenty of other times to read as well. I like to read at school before class starts. This helps calm myself down and get into the mood of learning.

Favorite Books

I have alot of favorites, since I love to read it is hard to choose between them.

  • Anything to do with Star Wars, I own them all.
  • R.A. Salvatore is my favorite overall author, and I own all his books, some even signed.
  • My favorite topic of books is Science Fiction/Fantasy with a bit of dabbling in military history.

Why I like reading!

I love reading for many reasons. One of which is escapism. Books allow me to escape into worlds unheard of. It allows me to escape the mundance boredom of everyday life.

Another reason I love reading is imagination. I get to explore the imaginations of many different people, and what they are capable of creating. I get to see through the lenses of another person's mind.

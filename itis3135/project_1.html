Project: Surf Fishing!

Project Overview

  • Application Purpose: The purpose of this website it to introduce visitors to the joys and intricacies of surf fishing.
  • Intended Users: The users of this website will be people who are new to surf fishing.
  • Overview: This site will include 5 pages. A home page, a page about different fish, a page about famous fishing spots, a page about baits and a page about fishing knots.

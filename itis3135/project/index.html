Surf Fishing

Surf fishing is the practice of catching fish while standing on the shoreline of the ocean or wading into the ocean surf.

A general term, surf fishing may or may not include casting a lure or bait, and refers to all type of shore fishing.

Best Baits for Surf Fishing

Here is a list of the best live baits for surf fishing.

  • Shrimp: As stated above, everything in the water loves shrimp making it one of the best if not best baits to use. They best used fresh instead of frozen.
  • Sand Fleas: One of the best things about this bait is you can catch them yourself fresh. They are plentiful on the beach during anytime of the day.
  • Clams: This bait is best used alive. They are usually closed completely when found alive. It is best to find a store for this as it is not cost friendly to find them on your own.
  • Crabs: Crabs take some preparation for use as bait. They are quite useful when prepared properly as they can survive harsh conditions. Fish love them.
  • Cut Bait: This is useful in the fact that you can reuse the small fish you caught as bait. You can turn the small fish into large fish.

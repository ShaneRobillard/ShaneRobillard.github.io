About Blue Wolf Astronomy:

Blue Wolf Astronomy started in the year 1985 as a gathering of amateur astronomers.
We looked to the sky with our own telescopes and was mesmerized by what we saw.
To this day we are interested in welcoming anyone and everyone who wants to see what the universe looks like.

This site was created to facilitate the meetings and gatherings of our astronomy club.
We have listings of events, in-depth details of those events, and the ability to create new events.
We also have a contact page if you need to contact us about anything.
We welcome members to login, or sign-up to stay on top of news about the club.
